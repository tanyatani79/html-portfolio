The Best Movies According to Tanya

My top 3 movies of all time

Yeh Jawani hai Deewani

This movie captures the essence of youth, the pursuit of dreams, and the importance of cherishing friendships

Zindagi na milegi Dobara

ZNMD serves as a reminder to seize the opportunities and experiences that life presents because one may not get a second chance

Kedarnath

Kedarnath explores themes of love, faith, and the impact of environmental disasters on human lives
